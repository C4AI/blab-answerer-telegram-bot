Ex.:
/q What is my name?
/a QAGeneratorBot, the greatest
Questions and answers will be aligned in the same order they were sent. For example, if you type:
/a QAGeneratorBot, the greatest
/q What is my name?
/q What is the color of Telegram symbol?
/a blue
the resulting entries on the database will be {(What is this bot's name?, QAGeneratorBot, the greatest), (What is the color of Telegram symbol?, blue)}
